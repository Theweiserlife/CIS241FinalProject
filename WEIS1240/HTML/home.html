About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. This is a work in progress as I am a work in progress. I will be updating this site as I finish classes and obtain certificates along the way. I started my college career back in 2008 at Everett Community College for fine art Photography. Family obligations (namely a baby) got in the way of being able to finish up my degree. I am just a few credits shy of graduating; my goal is to finish that degree up in 2023.

Today I am currently enrolled in Edmonds College for Cyber Defense and Digital Forensics. I didn’t just select this degree path, it kind of selected me. I was originally signed up for Web Application and Cloud Development. While completing the curriculum for web design I was invited to a lecture given by a former student who now works for Washington State Attorney General’s Office. Rebecca’s talk was transforming and inspiring, I wanted to know more and learn more about cyber security and how to do it. So that led me to take an introductory class called Digital Forensics and the Law. After that, I was hooked and halfway through the quarter changed my major committing myself to the study of digital forensics and cyber defense.

In my spare time, I am a mother of four great kids who keep me busy with all their activities. We are a Disney family and tend to make several trips a year to Disneyworld. Sometimes we sneak in a Disneyland trip if we are visiting family.

I am the main handyman and gardener around the house. I typically have three or four projects going at a time which keeps me busy, and it tends to be my stress relief. There is nothing better than getting your hands dirty and feeling like you have accomplished something at the end of the day. My list never gets shorter because I keep adding to it, I never want to run out of things to work on.

Thank you for visiting my page, feel free to look around and please link up with me if our interests line up. I am always looking for networking opportunities.
